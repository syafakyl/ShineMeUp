Samsung Galaxy Z Flip

The Samsung Galaxy Z Flip, also known by its secret nickname “Bloom” is a member of the Galaxy foldable phone series, similar to the Galaxy Fold except that the Flip can be folded vertically instead of horizontally. The Samsung Galaxy Z Flip was released on February 14, 2020 after being featured in a commercial at the 2020 Academy Awards.

Samsung Galaxy Z Flip features a hybrid glass layer, the “Infinity Flex Display,” the first foldable phone to have a partially glass screen (the rest is made entirely of plastic).

Samsung Galaxy S10

The Samsung Galaxy S10 is a smartphone released on March 8 2019. It weighs 157g and is 7.8mm thick. The phone runs on Android 9.0 and can be upgraded to Android 12 with One UI 4.1. It comes with a storage capacity of 128GB or 512GB, which can be expanded using microSDXC. This cellphone has a screen size of 6.1 inches with a resolution of 1440 x 3040 pixels. The phone's screen is made of Corning Gorilla Glass 6 and is always on.

The phone is powered by the Exynos 9820 chipset (8 nm) or Qualcomm SM8150 Snapdragon 855 chipset (7 nm), depending on the region. The phone has an octa-core CPU and a Mali-G76 MP12 or Adreno 640 GPU, depending on the chipset. This phone is equipped with 6 GB RAM or 8 GB RAM.
